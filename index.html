Dear Mama,

Happy Mother's Day po Mama!!! I miss you so much po 🫂🥺! Ito lang po kaya kong ibagay sayo ngayon dahil malayo ka, gusto ko pong malaman mo na miss na miss at mahal na mahal po kita kahit nasa ibang parte ka po ng mundo. Kaya ito po, gumawa po ako ng website gamit mga natututunan ko sa pag-aaral ko. Alam ko pong matagal-tagal na din po kita hindi na cha-chat at nakaka usap, and I'm sorry for that, talagang sobrang busy ko lang po ngayong second semester namin. Pero hinding hindi po kita makakalimutan 💖. I wanted to take a moment to tell you just how much you mean to me. Every time i feel down and sad, I still look for my mother. I've always wanted your comfort and presence just to feel not alone. I wanted to be strong like you. There is no other way to possibly repay the sacrifice you're doing for us. All I know is you're the strongest person I know, working tirelessly in another place far away from us and you're still standing no matter what. Wala po kami ngayon kung wala ka po. You have given us more than we asked for. I want to tell you that don't forget about yourself too mama, don't ever forget to eat right and rest if it's too much, you're still a person mama. Don't worry about us here, we're doing well all because of you. I love you more than words can ever say, but I hope this little surprise made you smile. 🌸🥰 I can't wait to see you again!! Love you mama!😘 With every piece of my heart,

- Von 💕
